Redesign the testimonials section into a premium trust-building section.

PROBLEM:
- Current testimonial cards look too basic and empty.
- Too much unused space inside the boxes.
- Testimonials feel generic.
- Section does not feel as premium as the rest of the website.

GOAL:
Make this section feel high-end, credible, modern, and conversion-focused.

NEW DESIGN:
Create a premium testimonial section with:
- 3 testimonial cards
- Client avatar circle or initials badge
- Star rating
- Short result tag
- Cleaner quote layout
- Premium card hover glow

CARD STRUCTURE:
Each card should include:

1. Top row:
- Client initials inside a gold circle
- Client name
- Business type
- 5 small gold stars

2. Middle:
- Quote text

3. Bottom:
- Result badge, example:
  “+60% κρατήσεις”
  “Brand identity upgrade”
  “2x ROAS”

DESIGN STYLE:
- Dark card background: #111111
- Thin gold border: rgba(212,175,55,0.15)
- Border radius: 14px
- Padding: 32px
- Gold accents only for stars, initials, and result badge
- Remove oversized quote icons or make them subtle in the background

ANIMATION:
- On hover:
  - card lifts slightly
  - border becomes gold
  - soft gold glow appears
  - stars glow subtly
